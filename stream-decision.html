What is stream selection in Ontario?
In Ontario, students entering Grade 9 choose a pathway that determines which courses they take across Grade 9–12. The choice affects university admission requirements, course difficulty, and available programs. Most newcomer families don't fully understand the system — this tool gives you a clear recommendation based on your child's actual situation.
