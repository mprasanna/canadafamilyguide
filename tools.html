What you actually pay
Marginal vs Effective — simply: You don't pay your top rate on everything. Canada uses brackets — 15% on the first $57k, 20.5% on the next chunk, and so on. Your effective rate is the real average. Most people earning $80–100k pay an effective combined rate of 28–33%, not 43%.
